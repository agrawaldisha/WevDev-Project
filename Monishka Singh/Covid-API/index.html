A GLANCE AT THE PANDEMIC

Stay Informed, Stay Safe

PREVENTION IS BETTER THAN CURE

WASHING HANDS PROPERLY

Maintaing hygiene by washing hands properly is something that not only protect you from coronavirus, but also a lot of other diseases.

MAINTAIN SOCIAL DISTANCING

Maintain a distance of atleast 6-feet while talking to someone to avoid the spread.

MASK IS MANDATORY

Wearing a mask is the new noem for this nocturnal generation to protect oneself, follow the same.

GET VACCINATED

Getting yourself vaccinated is more or less the most efficient way to fight this pandemic, so do not delay in doing so.

AVOID CROWDED PLACES

Avoid going to social gathering in this period, unless it is very important.

FOLLOW GOVERNMENT GUIDELINES

The safety rules and restrictions imposed by the government during this period are for your own good, so one should abide by them.
